About

With several enthusiastic students there are a lot of projects going on in IIT BHU. DIH or Design and Innovation Hub encourages students to take up projects.

DIH

The design and Innovation hub was formed in this institute in April 2014 (notified on 15 th April 2014) as an outcome of an exhaustive workshop on “Design and Innovation” which was attended by academic thinkers, design folk, grass roots inventors, scientist and engineers, social activists and people from humanities.

The Philosophy of Design and Innovation Hub:

The goal of education is to produce students with three qualities:

  1. Analytical ability (ability to analyse given situations),
  2. Building ability (ability to design with creativity), and
  3. Caring and character (sensitivity to others and courage to act on one’s beliefs)

These are the ABC of education. A and B pertain to “how to do” and C pertains to “what” and “why” of life. Many students, when they enter engineering, are full of enthusiasm to understand new areas, to build systems and to experiment and play with them. The primary goal of the Design and Innovation Hub (DIH) is to tap this enthusiasm and to direct it to exploration and sustained pursuit by the student which may result in development of a deeper interest in the chosen area or topic of study. The students would be encouraged to do research and undertake design and build things he or she dreams, thus converting his hard work into a journey of joy. The DIH also encourages faculty members to blend the creativity and zeal of students with their own rich experience and come up solutions on societal or industrial problems.


Working of Design and Innovation Hub:

Design and Innovation Hub would sponsor the following types of projects:

  1. Summer projects that can be undertaken by students in their summer vacations
  2. Projects that are carried out by students to compete in international events or events of repute.
  3. Extended projects (can be continued as a B Tech Project)
