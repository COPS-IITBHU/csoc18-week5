About

With a legacy spanning almost a hundred years, IIT BHU has given the world individuals, who with their virtues and flair have left impeccable footprints on the sands of time. Here are some of the noteworthy alumni who have achieved great things in life.

NIKESH ARORA

Former google executive and current President and COO of the Japanese $70 billion giant SoftBank Corp.




Dr.RAM CHARAN

He consistently ranked in the Thinkers50 Top Global Business Thinkers ranking from 2005-2009 & was the Economic Times Global Indian of the year in 2009. He was inducted into the Thinkers50 Hall of Fame in 2015.

DEEPAK AHUJHA

He has been named 'CFO of the Year' by the Silicon Valley Business Journal in the year 2010




MANOJ SINHA

Currently the Minister of State for Railways. Popularly known for his work in backward villages. Among the seven most honest MPs according to India Today.


PULICKEL AJAYAN

He is a pioneer in the field of nanotechnology and mentioned twice in Guinness World Records for making the smallest brush and also the darkest material on Earth which reflects only 0.045% of light.

THOMAS S.ANANTHARAMAN

He was a part of the team that built the IBM Deep Blue, a chess-playing computer which was the first to beat the World Champion Garry Kasparov.

ANIL BHARDWAJ

Dr. Anil Bhardwaj was inducted in the hall of fame of Distinguished Alumnus Awardees of IIT BHU as a token of appreciation for a wonderful career and an unparalleled contribution to India space research.

JAY CHAUDHARY

Founder of successful companies like Zscaler, AirDefense, CipherTrust etc. Conferred with Entrepreneur of the Year and Innovator and Influencer.


VIKAS PUROHIT

Ex-Amazon fashion head appointed as Vice-president for Paytm payment bank business.

V.K BANSAL

He single handedly created Bansal Classes, which at one point had a complete dominance over IIT-JEE results.He has created an entire industry by himself, drastically changing the Indian pre- engineering education landscape.

SRIDHAR MANTHANI

He is the Senior Director at NVIDIA's Bangalore Design Centre.He built Nvidia India from scratch and developed complete high performance chips from end to end while being based in India.

(Late) SHRI KRISHAN KANT

He worked as a Scientist with the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, New Delhi. He was a part of the Quit India movement and the Independence struggle as a youth and continued to be involved in politics, eventually being elected to the Parliament of India.
